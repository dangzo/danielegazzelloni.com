Professional Profile

Hello, my name is Daniele Gazzelloni, an Italian web application developer who set up his base in Canary Islands, Spain.

I started my journey specializing in CSS, PHP and Javascript-based technologies, like Node.js and the MEAN stack. When I discovered the beauty of Meteor.js I started to deeply focusing on it. I now have +2 years of commercial experience in Meteor and frontend development, creating web and mobile products from scratch.

I graduated in Computer Engineering in 2012 and I work remotely with startup and companies located all around the globe since then.

I follow lean and agile software development methods communicating with my clients daily to keep them always updated.

My work style is focused and detail oriented where code quality, low distractions and working within deadlines are my key elements.

I fluently speak English, Spanish and Italian. In my free time I love to stay fit going running, rock climbing and generally being in contact with nature (we developers kind of need this second life!).

I will only take in consideration remote job opportunities.
